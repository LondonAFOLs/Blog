Habitat Meetup - June 2020

The theme of this monthâ€™s meet-up was minifig habitats. Minifig habitats are 8x8x8 vignettes built around a particular minifig or set of minifigs and built in a way that allows them to be interlocked and stacked together.

Our resident leader of the habitat movement, Caz Mockett kicked things off by showing her extensive collection of habitats. We spent the rest of the meet-up socialising, building our own habitats and finishing with a show-and-tell.
