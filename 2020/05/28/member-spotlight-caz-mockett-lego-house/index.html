Member Spotlight: Caz Mockett - Micropolis and Lego House

Here Stuart Harris, Master Builder of the Lego House, interviews Caz.

Another of our members, Matt Elder, also interviewed Caz recently about being chosen for the Lego House Masterpiece Gallery.
